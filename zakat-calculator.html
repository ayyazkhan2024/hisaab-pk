Zakat Kaise Calculate Hoti Hai?

Zakat Islam ka teesra rukn hai aur har sahib-e-nisab Muslim par har lunar saal mein ek baar wajib hoti hai. Iski rate aapki total zakatable wealth ka 2.5% hai.

Nisab Kya Hai?

Nisab woh minimum amount hai jis se zyada wealth hone par Zakat wajib ho jaati hai. Do tareeqay hain calculate karne ke:

  • Gold Nisab: 87.48 grams (7.5 tola) gold ki value
  • Silver Nisab: 612.36 grams (52.5 tola) silver ki value

Zyada tar scholars silver ka nisab use karne ki tarjeeh dete hain kyunki ye lower hai aur zyada log Zakat dene ke qabil ban jaate hain.

Kis Par Zakat Wajib Hai?

  • Gold aur silver jo ek lunar saal se zyada waqt se aapke paas hai
  • Cash aur bank balance
  • Business stock aur investments ki market value
  • Shares aur stocks (agar trading ke liye rakhe hain)

Kis Par Zakat Wajib Nahi?

  • Rehne wala ghar (jo aap khud use kar rahe hain)
  • Personal use ki gaari
  • Rozmarra ki zaroorat ka samaan

Calculation Ka Formula

Total Zakatable Assets minus Liabilities, agar nisab se zyada ho, to us amount ka 2.5% Zakat wajib hai.
