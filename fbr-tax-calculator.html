FBR Tax Slabs 2025-26 (Salaried Persons)

Pakistan mein progressive tax system hai — matlab har slab ki rate sirf us slab ke andar wali income par lagti hai, poori income par nahi.

Official Slabs

  • 0% — Annual income up to PKR 600,000 (tax-free)
  • 2.5% — PKR 600,000 se 1,200,000 tak
  • 11% plus PKR 6,000 — PKR 1,200,000 se 2,200,000 tak
  • 23% plus PKR 116,000 — PKR 2,200,000 se 3,200,000 tak
  • 30% plus PKR 346,000 — PKR 3,200,000 se 4,100,000 tak
  • 35% plus PKR 616,000 — PKR 4,100,000 se zyada

Calculation Kaise Hoti Hai?

Maan len aapki annual salary PKR 1,800,000 hai. Ye 1,200,000–2,200,000 ke slab mein aati hai:

  • Fixed tax (pehle slabs ka) = PKR 6,000
  • Extra income us slab mein = 1,800,000 - 1,200,000 = PKR 600,000
  • Is par 11% tax = PKR 66,000
  • Total tax = PKR 72,000 per year

Zaroori Baat

Ye calculator estimate ke liye hai. Actual tax deduction mein aapki employer ki taraf se diye allowances, medical, aur other exemptions shamil ho sakti hain. Final confirmation ke liye FBR Iris portal ya tax consultant se rabta karen.
